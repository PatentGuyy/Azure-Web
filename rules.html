List of all Minecraft and Discord server rules.

Ignorance of the rules is no excuse!

Minecraft server

  1. Respect Others: Treat all players with kindness and respect. Harassment, discrimination, or offensive behavior will not be tolerated.
  2. No Griefing: Do not destroy or alter other players' builds or property without permission.
  3. No Cheating: Use of hacks, mods, or exploits that give an unfair advantage is prohibited.
  4. Follow the Server's Lore and Theme: Respect any lore, themes, or server-wide events. Stay within the boundaries of the server's setting.
  5. No Spamming or Advertising: Do not spam chat or advertise other servers or products.
  6. Use Appropriate Language: Keep chat clean and avoid inappropriate or offensive language.
  7. Respect Staff Decisions: Follow instructions from server staff and moderators. If you have concerns, address them respectfully.
  8. No Duplication Glitches: Exploiting duplication glitches or bugs is forbidden. Report any found to staff immediately.
