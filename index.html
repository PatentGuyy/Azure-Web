Server FAQs.

Find answers to common questions about our server, from joining and rules to how you can support us. Get all the info you need for a smooth and enjoyable experience!

What makes our Minecraft server unique?
Our server offers a vibrant community, custom plugins, and diverse gameplay modes like survival and Skyblock, ensuring a unique and engaging experience.
How can I join the server?
Simply add our server IP to your Minecraft client and connect. Detailed instructions are available on our website.
Are there any rules I need to follow?
Yes, we have a set of rules to maintain a friendly and fair environment. You can find them on our server's website or in the in-game /rules command.
How can I support the server?
You can support us by voting for the server, donating, or purchasing items from our online store. Every bit helps us improve and grow the community.
